Q&A to Quizlet Converter

How to Use
  1. Copy Q&A data (two columns)
  2. Paste below
  3. Click "Format for Quizlet"
  4. Review and copy result
  5. Go to Quizlet
  6. Click "+ Import"
  7. Paste and set delimiters:
    • Term/definition: Comma
    • Between cards: Semicolon

Note: Commas in text are converted to ". " automatically
